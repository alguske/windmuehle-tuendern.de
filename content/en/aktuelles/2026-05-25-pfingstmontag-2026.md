On Whit Monday many people came together at the Windmühle Tündern. In wonderful summer weather it turned into a relaxed, easy-going afternoon in the mill garden.

## A garden party in the shade of the mill

Long beer benches under the trees, a full garden, good spirits. Many people from Tündern came, along with guests from the surrounding area.
