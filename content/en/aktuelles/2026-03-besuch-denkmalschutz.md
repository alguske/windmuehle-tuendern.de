We had a visit from experts of the German Foundation for Monument Protection (Deutsche Stiftung Denkmalschutz), who came to inspect the current progress of the restoration work on the windmill.

The experts were very satisfied with the progress of the work. Their positive feedback confirms that the restoration is on the right track.

    <p>We thank the German Foundation for Monument Protection for their commitment and support in preserving our windmill.</p>
    <p>The German Foundation for Monument Protection is one of the most important supporters of our project. Together with the EU (LEADER), the state, the district of Hameln-Pyrmont, the Calenberg-Grubenhagen Landscape Association and the city of Hameln, they make this extensive restoration possible. This visit underscores the significance of the Tündern Windmill as a cultural monument worth preserving.</p>
    <p>The windmill is not just a technical monument, but a living piece of local history. Thanks to the combination of public funding, private donations and voluntary commitment, the goal of preserving the mill for future generations is coming closer step by step.</p>
