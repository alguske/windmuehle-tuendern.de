Dear members, sponsors, supporters, construction workers and craftsmen,

The year is ending. Our "Windmill" project has made good progress.

The plastering work is done and the new windows are installed. The mill is now waiting for the new coat of paint. If the weather holds, the painting will begin early in the new year. The new sails are already made. They will be installed after the scaffolding comes down.

We thank all the craftsmen and companies for their work.

We thank you all for your support and wish you and your families a Merry Christmas and a good new year.
