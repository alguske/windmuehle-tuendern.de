We are very grateful for the generous donation from the **Basarteam Tündern**. Members of the bazaar team and the Förderverein Windmühle Tündern e.V. came together for the handover.

The proceeds from the bazaar go directly into our work at the windmill. They help us keep the mill in good shape and open for guided tours and events.

Many thanks to the Basarteam and to everyone who visited the bazaar and supported our windmill.
