On 17 April we celebrated the inauguration of our windmill in Tündern. The weather was kind and many guests joined us.

The mill was officially inaugurated at the decorated podium. We thanked all the craftsmen, supporters and helpers who made the project possible.

There was live music from the volunteer fire brigade's band right next to the mill. From above you could take in the whole celebration: the mill in the middle, the guests and the green marquee all around.

Thank you to all visitors for the good wishes and for the donations, large and small. They help us keep the Tündern windmill in good shape and continue to open it for tours and events.
