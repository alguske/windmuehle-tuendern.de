  <strong>Help keep the Tündern windmill alive.</strong>
  <p>Your donation supports the preservation, care and further development of our historic windmill. Every contribution helps us bring history to life, also for generations to come.</p>
</div>

<section class="content-section">

### Restoration complete

The major restoration was budgeted at **€166,600** and was made possible by funding from the **EU (LEADER)**, the **State of Lower Saxony**, the **Hameln-Pyrmont District**, **Calenberg-Grubenhagensche Landschaft**, the **City of Hameln** and the **German Foundation for Monument Protection**. The inauguration took place on 17 April 2026.

</section>

<section class="content-section">

### What your donation funds now

The windmill stands tall again, but the work continues. Your donation goes directly into:

- Ongoing care and small repairs to the building
- Work on the outdoor area around the mill
- Construction of a sanitary facility
- Repairs and renovation inside the mill
- Guided tours and events for visitors
- Development of an educational concept for guided visits

</section>
